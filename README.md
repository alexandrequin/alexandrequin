## Hi there 👋

I’m Alexandre Quin, a freelance web developer specializing in Full Stack JavaScript Node.js.

I have 2 years of experience as a Full Stack WordPress Developer. After that, I completed a Web Developer Designer training program, where I strengthened my skills in front-end and back-end development, software architecture, and database management. This training allowed me to refine my expertise in web technologies and adopt best development practices.  
Today, I am a Full Stack JavaScript Node.js Developer, specializing in back-end development, and I work as a freelancer.

💻 I love designing APIs and microservices with Node.js, always exploring best practices in back-end development. I’m also a big fan of open-source projects and enjoy tinkering with Linux servers to optimize my work environment.

⚙️ Passionate about DevOps, I love automating my tasks to improve productivity and reliability. Whether through scripts, CI/CD pipelines, or orchestration tools, I’m always looking for ways to optimize my development and deployment workflows.

🤖 Fascinated by artificial intelligence, I enjoy experimenting and exploring the latest advancements in AI.

🎮 Besides coding, I’m a huge fan of manga and video games. There’s nothing better than spending dark nights reading code and fine-tuning my projects.

🚀 Always seeking to learn and improve, I’m open to discussions and collaborations!
